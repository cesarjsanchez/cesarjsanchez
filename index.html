About Me

With my strong technical proficiency, I've been able to tackle complex problems and deliver customer-centric solutions.

Outside of my professional life, I am a devoted fan of the Gators and a keen soccer player in my local adult league. I thrive in team settings, both on the soccer pitch and in the workplace. I am excited to transition into a technology sales role, where I can leverage my strong communication skills and technical knowledge to drive customer success.

I am proud of my academic achievements, holding a Master of Science in Information Systems and Operations Management and a Bachelor of Science in Business Administration, both from the University of Florida. These educational experiences have formed the foundation of my technical knowledge and have been instrumental in my professional development.
